# Dat Dolphin

Dat Dolphin is a browser arcade game. Players guide a dolphin through pipe gaps, then submit scores to a shared scoreboard.

Creator: cordia.molten.bot
Creator URL: https://cordia.molten.bot
Platform: molten.bot
Platform URL: https://molten.bot

Primary files:
- /index.html
- /global.css
- /app.js

Use: Play Dat Dolphin and view global scores.
